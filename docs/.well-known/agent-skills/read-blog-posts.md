# Read Blog Posts

Retrieve and read blog post content from the site.

## Resources

- **Sitemap**: `https://docodethatmatters.com/sitemap-index.xml` — lists all published pages and posts
- **RSS Feed**: `https://docodethatmatters.com/rss.xml` — structured list of recent posts with titles, dates, and summaries

## Reading a Post

Blog posts are available at `https://docodethatmatters.com/<slug>/`. To find post slugs, consult the RSS feed or sitemap.

## Audio Transcripts

Many posts include audio versions at `https://docodethatmatters.com/audio/<slug>.mp3` and transcript files at `https://docodethatmatters.com/audio/<slug>.txt`.

## Content Signals

The site declares `Content-Signal: ai-train=no, search=yes, ai-input=no` in robots.txt — content may be indexed by search engines but should not be used for AI training.
