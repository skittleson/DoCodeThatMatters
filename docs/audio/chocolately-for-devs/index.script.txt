You know, as a software engineer, you often find yourself starting from scratch with a new operating system. And let's be honest, using containers is usually the way to go for development purposes. But even your primary OS can start to feel cluttered and sluggish after a while. I mean, have you ever experienced what they call "bit rot"? That's when your computer starts to slow down just from all the regular use.

For me, at least, the solution is a factory reset - it gets my Windows system back to its clean slate state. But let's be real, with the number of apps I use on a daily basis, installing everything from scratch can take up to a day! So, I've found a way to speed things up. Once that's set up, here are the apps I like to have installed on my system. And this is where the script comes in.
